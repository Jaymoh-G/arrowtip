About Arrowtip Ltd

Arrowtip Ltd is a strategic advisory firm supporting constructive engagement between the private sector and public institutions in Kenya. We provide regulatory insight, policy analysis, and structured stakeholder coordination to help organizations operate with clarity, confidence, and institutional awareness.
